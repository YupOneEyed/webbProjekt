Beginner's Guide
How to play and improve:
When you slice a bloq you can recieve a maximum of 115 points, that are split into two parts: the swing angle (100pts) and the accuracy (15pts). The swing angle required for the 100 points is 100 degrees before the bloq and 60 degrees after. For the 15 accuracy points, simple try to hit as close to the middle of the bloq as possible, which will give you 15 points if you're dead on.
If you see yourself missing alot, try playing slower maps and paying more attention to where the actual bloqs are. It's easy to find yourself swinging in the general direction of a bloq and therefore either getting a bad cut (if the angle is off or if you hit the wrong side) or simply missing it entirely. After you've completed a map with only a few misses, or even full combo'd a map, you can play increasingly difficult maps and improve at those.
