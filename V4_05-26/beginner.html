Beginner's Guide
How to play:
When you slice a bloq you can recieve a maximum of 115 points, that are split into two parts: the swing angle (100pts) and the accuracy (15pts). The swing angle required for the 100 points is 100 degrees before the bloq and 60 degrees after. For the 15 accuracy points, simple try to hit as close to the middle of the bloq as possible, which will give you 15 points if you're dead on.
